Main Pre-Review Risks

HighStudy design is underspecified

Clarify randomization, blinding, comparator, and whether the trial is single-arm or controlled.

HighECOG rule is not operational

Define accepted ECOG performance status range and documentation method.

HighMeasurable disease rule is missing

Clarify whether measurable disease is required and which response assessment standard applies.

HighBiomarker rules are unclear

Define required biomarker testing, exclusions, and handling of missing results.

MediumRecruitment rationale is thin

Add screening pool, historical recruitment rate, or site feasibility evidence.

MediumFollow-up attendance needs definition

Define visit windows, acceptable missed visits, remote options, and feasibility checks.
